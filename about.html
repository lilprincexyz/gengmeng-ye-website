About Me

Gengmeng Ye

Data Analyst


I didn't expect a quantitative investment internship to change the way I think, but it did.

Coming from a finance background, I was used to relying on theories and experience. Then I started building models, and the data kept challenging what I thought I knew. Somewhere along the way, I realized I enjoyed being proven wrong. That curiosity is what pulled me into analytics, and it's still what keeps me here.

Today, I work with Google Ads data and still get the same satisfaction when I uncover something unexpected. Every campaign tells a different story, and I like figuring out why.

That curiosity doesn't stop when I close my laptop. It's probably why I've jumped out of a plane, why kite surfing is next on my list, and why I can lose track of time in an art museum. Visual storytelling has shaped the way I think about dashboards more than I ever expected.

I also laugh a lot. I take my work seriously, but not myself. The best teams I've been part of solved difficult problems while enjoying the process, and that's the kind of teammate I hope to be.

If any of this resonates with you, I'd love to connect on LinkedIn. Whether you'd like to discuss a project, exchange ideas, or simply say hello, feel free to message via LinkedIn or email me.
